About Me

Hi! I'm Kazibwe Francis, a passionate software engineer currently pursuing a degree in Software Engineering at Makerere University. With a strong foundation in programming and a keen interest in mobile and web development, I thrive on turning innovative ideas into reality.

My journey into the tech world began at a young age, where I was captivated by the power of technology to solve real-world problems. Since then, I have dedicated my academic and professional efforts to mastering a diverse range of programming languages and frameworks, including:

  • Dart: As a core language for Flutter, Dart enables me to build high-performance, cross-platform applications with ease.
  • Flutter: I enjoy developing mobile applications that provide seamless user experiences, utilizing Flutter's rich widget library and powerful capabilities.
  • Laravel: With Laravel, I create robust and scalable web applications, leveraging its elegant syntax and extensive ecosystem to build APIs and manage backend functionalities.
  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: These foundational web technologies allow me to create visually appealing and interactive websites that engage users effectively.

In addition to my technical skills, I have a strong understanding of data structures and algorithms, which helps me optimize code efficiency and problem-solving capabilities. My coursework in Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) and AI has further fueled my interest in building intelligent applications that can adapt and learn from user interactions.

Beyond coding, I enjoy collaborating on projects with fellow developers, contributing to open-source initiatives, and staying updated with the latest industry trends. I have worked on various projects, including:

  • My AI Nanny: A Flutter-based application designed to assist children in learning sounds and naming objects, complete with a ranking system to track performance.
  • eCommerce Web Application: Developed using Laravel, this project features a fully functional online store, integrating payment gateways and user-friendly interfaces.

As a member of the Makerere University community, I actively participate in hackathons and coding challenges, continuously honing my skills and expanding my network. I believe in the importance of lifelong learning and am always eager to explore new technologies and frameworks.

In my free time, I enjoy reading tech blogs, watching programming tutorials, and experimenting with new tools and languages. I'm particularly fascinated by the potential of data science and machine learning to revolutionize industries and improve decision-making processes.

Feel free to reach out if you'd like to connect or discuss potential collaborations. I'm always open to new opportunities and challenges!
